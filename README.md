Angry Frog Bussiness (game)
	The game about jumping at mosqitoes to eat them, and then play Frog Casino with said mosqitoes.
Game mechanics:
- 	Jumping and sticking - you choose the angle and the strength of the jump, and when you land, you fall aroud until your legs stick to something.
- 	Catching - when you go through a mosqitoe, you add it to your inventory.
- 	Mosqitoe spawning - mosqitos will spawn from the sides and the top of the screen, and will randomly fly with a chance to notice you (random), when they do, they will go 
	towards you and if they bite you before you eat it, you die and end the level. With time more mosqitoes will spawn
- 	Gameplay loop - After you are overwhelmed by mosqitoes, you go to casino to use them to try to win the game, until you lose all your mosqitoes and go back to the swamp.
